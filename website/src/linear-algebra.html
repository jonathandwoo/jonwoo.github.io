Linear Algebra is the study of linear equations and their representations in vector spaces and matrices (Wikipedia). It is very useful in the context of science and engineering as well as for other mathematical subjects such as differential equations and machine learning. It is often the starting place for a rigorous study of mathematics. The notes included below are compiled from Linear Algebra by Kenneth Hoffman and Ray Kunze.
